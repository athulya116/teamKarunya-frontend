About Us

Welcome to Team Karunya

Team Karunya is a leading and trusted provider of essential services including 24/7 ambulance services, mobile mortuary transportation, and a wide range of event hire services in Kerala. Our commitment lies in delivering top-quality, dependable, and compassionate support for emergencies, public events, and personal functions.

Our ambulance services are available around the clock and are equipped with modern facilities like oxygen supply, basic life support (BLS) systems, and first-aid kits to ensure rapid medical response during accidents, critical care transfers, and maternity support. We take pride in offering timely, professional, and affordable emergency medical care.

In addition to medical transport, Team Karunya offers mobile mortuary services that provide respectful and dignified preservation and transportation of deceased individuals. Our facilities are well-equipped to meet the cultural and logistical needs of families during times of loss.

Our event equipment hire services include everything you need to organize successful functions like weddings, funerals, birthdays, engagements, and more. We provide professional audio systems, LED walls, stage lights, shamiyana tents, tarpaulins, generators, fans, chairs, and tables. Our team ensures on-time delivery, proper setup, and dedicated customer service to make every occasion stress-free and memorable.

Choose Team Karunya for the best ambulance services, mobile mortuary support, and event equipment rentals in Kerala. We combine professionalism, reliability, and compassion to serve our community with excellence.
